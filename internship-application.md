Use this form for internships, visiting students/scholars, postdoc or fellowship enquiries, and KCL MSc/BSc project interest.

## What to include in your enquiry

- Current status (degree programme, institution, or current role)
- Areas of interest and preferred project direction
- Practical evidence of fit: coursework, projects, publications, or repositories
- Timeline and funding source (especially for visiting applicants)

## Typical routes covered by this form

- KCL MSc and BSc projects
- Visiting students and scholars
- Research internships
- Postdoc and fellowship exploratory enquiries
