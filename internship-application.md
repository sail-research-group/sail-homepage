# Internship & Research Role Application

Thank you for your interest in applying for an **internship or research role** with the SAIL Research Group at King's College London.

## About the Positions

We offer **MSc and BSc thesis projects** and research internships for students on topics such as:

- Implementing simplified **PIM-friendly kernels** (e.g., GEMV, KV caches)
- Building **prototypes and tools** for memory/storage profiling
- Small-scale **AI/bioinformatics accelerators** using CPU/GPU/FPGA

We also welcome applications from **visiting students and scholars**.

### What we are looking for

- Interest in computer architecture, systems, or related areas
- Programming experience (C/C++, Python, or other languages)
- Strong motivation and willingness to learn
- Ability to work in a research environment

### Application Process

If you are a current **KCL student**:
- Check the official project allocation system (when available).
- Or fill out the application form below

For external applicants and visiting students, please fill out the form below.
